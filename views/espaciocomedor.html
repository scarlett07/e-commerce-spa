Basic tips to decorate your dinning room

Bright ideas!

Lighting is a key feature of any room and should ideally provide a range of lighting options for every mood. Ample brightness is perfect for helping out with homework, while soft lighting is much better at creating an intimate dining atmosphere.

Learn More

Work the room

A dining room without a dining room table is, well, just a room. So make sure that your lighting plan compliments your dining furniture – after all, it is the room where you’ll be making memories with loved ones for years to come.

Learn More

Decorate the walls

Pro tip: Never, ever, ever underestimate the impact that your wall color and décor can have on the room. Not only is it some of the largest real estate in any space, it’s the perfect opportunity for you to make the dining room feel truly your own.
